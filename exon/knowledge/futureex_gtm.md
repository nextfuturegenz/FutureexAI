SECTION 1: THE CATEGORY
The Single Most Important Sentence
text
FutureEx is not a Generative AI company.
FutureEx is an Executive AI company.
Why This Distinction Wins
Generative AI → makes stuff (copy, images, code, answers)
Executive AI → makes decisions and executes them

Every other "AI for business" company is Generative AI in costume:

Jasper, Copy.ai, Writer → generate copy
ChatGPT Enterprise, Glean → generate answers
Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot → generate documents
Even most "agentic" startups → mostly generate-then-maybe-do
Nobody owns "Executive AI" yet. The term is empty real estate. Whoever names it, owns it.

What "Executive AI" Communicates Instantly
When a Founder hears "Executive AI," they think:

✅ This is not a writing tool
✅ This makes calls, not just suggestions
✅ This has authority, not just opinions
✅ This runs things, not just describes them
That entire framing happens in 2 seconds, before they've heard the demo.
